Extraction and Next Steps

Now that we have enough DNA, all that is left to do is extract their DNA in order to study it!

DNA Extraction

To collect the DNA inside the capsid (head) of the phage, we have to break it! We use special enzymes called DNase and RNase enzymes to get rid of any bacterial DNA, and then use DNA clean-up Resin to dissolve the capsid.
